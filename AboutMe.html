About Me

Started as a pro-triathlete I suffered from some physical discomfort so I had to end my career. From then on my transformation to become a game developer began. It showed I had some talents for developing things. Because I had to stop my sports career I became into the situation to ease of an inconvenience and start my study as a game developer at the University of the Arts in Utrecht (HKU). started a bit later than might be expected I am giving it a full throttle now.

Sports Highlights

European Championships

Two times qualifier and competitor in European championships. The first one was in Eilat (Israel) and the year after in Alanya (Turkey).

World Championships

Due to the results in the European championship I also qualified for the World championships on the Olympic venue in London (England).

Australian Youth Olympic Festival

This event took place in Sydney, Australia. As one of only four athletes I represented, together with my sister, the Netherlands on that event.

Developer Interests

Multiplayer

The reason why I am interested in multiplayer games is mainly because of the competition with and against other people. Besides that I am intrested in how games like "Call of Duty" and "Overwatch" manage the interaction between players. So my last few projects were mainly focused on creating online multiplayer games.

Mobile Platform

Since my freshman year I have been creating and working on mobile games. The main reason for me is that I think mobile games are easily accessible for almost everyone. And the mobile platform offers the necessary challenges in aswell hard- and software.
